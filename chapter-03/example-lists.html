Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite food. here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream and eggs
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. hwen the eggs are still moist remove frome the heat (it will continue to cook on the palte until served)
